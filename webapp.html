Screenshot Guide

Windows 10 / 11 (Recommended)

  1. Press Win + Shift + S to open Snip & Sketch
  2. Select the area of the screen you want to capture
  3. Come back here and paste with Ctrl + V or right-click → Paste in the text box

Windows 7 / 8

  1. Open Start Menu and search for "Snipping Tool"
  2. Click "New" and select the area to capture
  3. Save the screenshot, then use the Upload Image button below to upload it

macOS

  1. Press Cmd + Shift + 4 to capture a selected area
  2. The screenshot is saved to your desktop — use Upload Image to upload it
